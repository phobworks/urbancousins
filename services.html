Best Care Services

Services - Support. Dignity. Independence.

Discover our range of personalised care services designed to empower and uplift every individual we serve.

Home Care

Our carers will support you in your home with personal care and all activities of daily living and to ensure you live comfortably and safely. These may include supporting with:

  • Personal care & hygiene
  • Transferring from and into bed
  • Bath, Showering, Washing Assistance
  • Meal preparation
  • Feeding and drinking
  • Administering prescribed medication
  • Supporting to appointments

Our support will always be provided in a manner that respects and promotes your independence, choice and dignity.

Live-in Care

We provide live-in care (24hrs) for clients with physical disability, frailty or dementia by providing assistance with activities of daily living, medication management, monitoring and supervision to ensure and maintain your safety in your own home rather than going into a Care Home.

Reablement

We support Adult social care and NHS in providing Reablement services in person's own home to help them to regain their skills and confidence so they can learn to manage again after a period if illness. We provide this service using Consistent team of workers to enable a relationship to be built with the person and for progress to be properly monitored

Blitz Cleaning Services

Our agency provides one-off deep cleaning or a regular/routine cleaning service, and we are happy to cater for all your domestic cleaning needs, including ironing, dusting, polishing and vacuuming.

Sitting Service

Our sitting service provides flexible short term, time-limited monitoring, supervision and companionship to our service users to give their carer/s a relief in their caring role

Domestic Support

Our carers can assist with your domestic duties such as your cleaning, cooking, gardening and shopping.

Escort Services

Our carers can accompany you and assist you with your outdoor tasks and activities like social & recreational activities as well as providing you companionship if required.

Direct Payment

Our services cover both Local Authority commissioned servicesas well as direct payments where service users are arranging their own care and support.

Service User Groups

Elderly / Frail

We provide gentle, respectful support to older adults, helping them maintain dignity, independence, and a high quality of life in the comfort of their own homes.

Dementia

Our dementia care is tailored with patience and understanding, offering familiar routines, emotional reassurance, and a safe, supportive environment every step of the way.

Physical Disability

We deliver practical and empowering care that adapts to individual abilities, promoting independence, accessibility, and everyday well-being.

Mental Health

With empathy and discretion, we support adults living with mental health conditions, focusing on stability, confidence, and a path toward personal goals.

Eating Disorder

We offer sensitive, person-centred care for those affected by eating disorders, working alongside professionals to promote recovery, dignity, and emotional support.

Sensory Impairment

Our team is trained to support individuals with visual or hearing impairments through clear communication, adaptive tools, and respectful, responsive care.

We believe that everyone deserves to live comfortably and safely and we are committed to providing compassionate and reliable care tailored to individual needs in the comfort of their own homes.
